  • Tequila

  • The Classic Tequila Sunrise

    A pretty drink with layers of orange juice and grenadine . The ratio of juice to tequila is high makes it a good choice for a brunch cocktail.
    Drink Recipe:
    * 1.5 oz. Hornitos Repasado Tequila
    * 3/4 cup fresh orange juice
    * 1.5 fluid ounce) grenadine syrup
    * 1 slice orange, for garnish
    * 1 maraschino cherry for garnish Ice

  • Whiskey

  • The Manhattan (Rye)

    One of the great classic cocktails, made the old fashioned way -- with rye whiskey.
    Drink Recipe:
    * 1/4 cup Bulleit rye whiskey
    * 2 tablespoons sweet vermouth
    * 2 dashes Angostura bitters
    * 1 maraschino cherry

Wines

This month Elixi is featuring wines from Brack Mountain Wine Company.

Founded in 2008, Brack Mountain Wine Company is a next generation winery focused on producing hand-crafted, premium wines sourced from the best growers, offered at a fair price.

brackmountainwine.com

  • Chardonnay

  • Barrique

    Pale yellow hue in the glass, this wine opens with abundant layers of brioche, lemon zest and toasted hazelnut. The palate is creamy, textured with macadamia nut and traces of lemon meringue which lingers on the lengthy, mouthwatering finish. Approx. $18

  • Cabernet

  • Bench

    Deep, dark purple in color, this wine offers aromas of violets, huckleberry and a touch of vanilla spice. The palate is elegantly wound with dusty tannins and a supple, velvet texture. The seamless finish, ebbs and flows with flavors of black currant, dark cocoa and forest floor. Approx. $24
